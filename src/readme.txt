Final Project: Mech
by Arjen van der ende
-------------

FEATURES:
* Import of custom designed 3d-models
* Custom animation of the player model
* Interactive camera
* Texture mapping (inluding sphere-mapped textures)

CONTROLS:
mouse	- look around
lft btn	- prepare cannons for firing
w	- walk forward
a	- rotate left	(while walking)
d	- rotate right	(while walking)
space	- pause
f	- toggle fog
c	- toggle freelook
